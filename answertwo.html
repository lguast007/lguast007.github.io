Quick Tips

High-energy dogs come in all shapes and sizes! From playful giants,like Great Danes, to little racers, like a Jack Russell Terrier, you have a lot of options.
Picking a dog from this category will depend on how much time you have,
and how big a dog you're prepared to handle, especially because some of these breeds are highly intelligent! They will need more time from you.
